About Me

-

Hi, I'm Lennart Schaeffer. I'm a first year Computer Science student at Dalhousie, and so far I am enjoying my studies very much. I was born in Canada, but moved to an island called Curacao just before I turned two years old. I lived there for 13 years, and then moved back to Canada in 2020. I attended high school in Moncton, New Brunswick, where I started playing football and became very enthusiastic about fitness and working out. In my last year of high school, I took Computer Science 11 and 12 and became very interested in coding. Since then, I have loved to code, especially in the realm of developing web applications and websites. I hope to one day become a Full-Stack Software Engineer.
